My Favourite Movies


Five Feet Apart

Five Feet Apart is a romantic, heart-rending drama about doomed young love. It highlights the plight of patients living with cystic fibrosis and will appeal to adult and teenage audiences. Although the plot is fairly predictable, the acting is very good and the movie's messages are purposeful and empowering.


Fatherhood

It centres on Matt (Kevin Hart), a newlywed who, following the death of his wife Liz (Deborah Ayorinde), finds himself parenting his infant daughter by himself. He witnesses her terrible demise, which is heartbreaking. Liz's absence is felt throughout the whole film, even though it is about the struggles and victories of a single parent.


Top Gun: Maverick

As a top naval aviator thirty years later, Maverick is still pushing the boundaries. However, when he guides the elite graduates of TOP GUN on a mission that requires the utmost sacrifice from those selected to fly it, he must face the demons of his past.
